Case Studies

Systems shaped by user friction and operating constraints

E-commerce Platform

Checkout flow redesign for completion reliability

  • Problem: Drop-offs during checkout caused by form friction and unclear errors.
  • UX decisions: Inline validation, clearer field sequencing, and actionable error feedback.
  • Trade-offs: Optimized scripts and progressive enhancement to support weak connections.
  • Constraint focus: Low-bandwidth handling and resilient submit states on unstable networks.

Result: smoother conversion path and fewer failed checkout attempts.

Operations Dashboard

Admin experience built for non-technical teams

  • Problem: Staff needed to manage data without developer support.
  • UX decisions: Task-based navigation, guided actions, and safe defaults.
  • Trade-offs: Prioritized clarity over visual density for faster onboarding.
  • Constraint focus: Designed for mixed digital literacy and varied devices.

Cross-Platform Mobile App

Lightweight field workflow with offline-friendly behavior

  • Problem: Field teams lost progress during intermittent connectivity.
  • UX decisions: Local-first interactions and delayed sync patterns.
  • Trade-offs: Kept UI minimal to improve speed on lower-end phones.
  • Constraint focus: Practical navigation and feedback for real-world usage.
